Instructions

Hello, Welcome to my Game,
    Player 1 (left character) controls on keyboard:
  • k/K: Used to perform Kick action
  • p/P: Used to perform Punch action
  • b/B: Used to perform Block action
  • d/D: Used to move the (left)character to the right (forwards action)
  • a/A: Used to move the (left)character to the left (backwards action)

    Player 2 (right character) controls on keyboard:
  • 7: Used to perform Kick action
  • 9: Used to perform Punch action
  • 5: Used to perform Block action
  • Right Arrow: Used to move the (right)character to the right (backwards action)
  • Left Arrow: Used to move the (right)character to the left (forwards action)
