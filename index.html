Player 1 (left character) controls on keyboard:

  • k/K: Used to perform Kick action
  • p/P: Used to perform Punch action
  • b/B: Used to perform Block action
  • d/D: Used to move the (left)character to the right (move forward)
  • a/A: Used to move the (left)character to the left (move backward action)

Player 2 (right character) controls on keyboard:

  • 7: Used to perform Kick action
  • 9: Used to perform Punch action
  • 5: Used to perform Block action
  • Right Arrow →: Used to move the (right)character to the right (move backward action)
  • Left Arrow ←: Used to move the (right)character to the left (move forward)
